FILMOGRAPHY Documentary
Documentary · 2021

The New Normal

Changing Trends in the Pandemic

As lockdowns reshaped daily life, a quiet revolution unfolded in home kitchens across the city. This documentary follows the parallel stories of first-time homebakers who found purpose and livelihood through small, indie-style bakeries — and the established patisseries that had to reimagine everything they knew to survive.

A story about resilience, reinvention, and the universal comfort of bread.

Direction Deepica S, Ponni Suresh Theme Food · Pandemic · Entrepreneurship
